# Content review before publication

The prototype wording is a complete first draft, not an approved Trust policy document. Record agreed changes directly in the relevant module file.

## Chaplaincy team review

- Confirm the description of the shared UHL and LPT volunteer role.
- Confirm how volunteers should introduce themselves and offer a visit.
- Confirm the guidance on prayer, ritual and referral to faith representatives.
- Confirm the boundaries on gifts, personal contact, social media and post-discharge contact.
- Decide whether to retain the existing active-listening video.
- If the video remains, check its captions and replace the written alternative with an approved transcript or equivalent.
- Confirm the assessment pass mark and certificate wording.

## Trust or policy-owner review

- Confirm the wording about confidentiality and appropriate information-sharing.
- Confirm the immediate escalation route for suicidal language, risk and safeguarding disclosures.
- Confirm who a volunteer tells first in UHL and LPT settings where arrangements differ.
- Confirm the boundaries around clinical questions, complaints and practical assistance.
- Confirm current infection-prevention, dress-code, isolation and sickness guidance.
- Confirm that this course is described as chaplaincy-specific preparation rather than a replacement for mandatory Trust learning.

## Accessibility and publication review

- Test keyboard navigation, visible focus, screen-reader reading order and zoom to 200% and 400%.
- Test the course at common phone, tablet and desktop widths.
- Check colour contrast and the printable certificate.
- Check captions and a full written alternative for every retained video.
- Add an approved accessibility statement and a monitored contact route before the course goes live.
- Agree use of organisation names and any UHL, LPT or NHS branding.
- Replace “Prototype” and update the version and review date in `content/course.json`.
- Test on the final GitHub Pages address before replacing the existing course.

## Maintenance

- Nominate a content owner and review the course at an agreed interval.
- Update the visible review date after every approved content review.
- Run both validation scripts after editing.
- Recheck external video links whenever team membership or published media changes.
